Pumpkin Roll

"Pumpkin roll is the best dessert I've ever tasted"

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients given below
  2. Create the cake by adding the given ingredients and mixing thoroughly.
  3. Pour cake batter into a pan & let it cook for 13 minutes
  4. While waiting for the cake to finish, start creating the filling
  5. When the time is up for the cake take it out of the oven and roll it into a swirl form.
  6. Let the swirled cake sit in the refrigerator for 60 minutes to cool.
  7. After 60 minutes, unroll the cake and spread the filling thoroughly throughout.
  8. Finally, Roll the cake again into its final form. Enjoy!

Time & Temperature

  • Preheat oven to 375 degrees
    • Cook: 13 min
    • Cool: 60 min

Cake Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 3/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup pumpkin puree
Filling Ingredients
  • 1 8 ounce package of cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 cup powdered sugar, sifted
  • 6 tablespoons butter or margarine, softened
